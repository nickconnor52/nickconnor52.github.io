Dave Matthews

Dave Matthews is an influencial South African musician from the early 90's that helped to popularize and infuse a cohesive blend of Alternative & Acoustic Rock with a hint of blues. The Dave Matthews Band is one of the hardest working bands of the last 25 years, touring every summer since 1992, with the highest ticket sales of any band in that time frame. This year, the band celebrates its 25th anniversary.

Dave Matthews Band Discography:

  • Crash: 1996
  • Before These Crowded Streets: 1998
  • Everyday: 2001
  • Busted Stuff: 2002
  • Some Devil: 2003 [Dave Matthews Solo Album]
  • Stand Up: 2005
  • Big Whiskey & the GrooGrux King: 2009
  • Away from the World: 2012
